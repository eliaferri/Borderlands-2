Originally from Hieronymous, Axton spent ten years with the Dahl military force, reaching the rank of sergeant. After his pursuit of personal glory and disregard for orders led to numerous compromised missions, his wife and commanding officer Sarah simultaneously divorced him and discharged him from the military. Sarah made it a point to highlight that this would lead to his death by firing squad, then ordered him not to flee to any of the numerous border worlds beyond Dahl's reach. Taking her not-so-subtle suggestion, Axton went AWOL, using his skills and turret as a mercenary on other planets. Axton became aware of the Vault thanks to a radio advertisement orchestrated by Handsome Jack, who had been monitoring the Commando's most recent bounty hunt. Although Axton was turning in more bounties and making far more money than anyone else in Sheriff Youngblood's jurisdiction, he found that it was too easy, and the lure of fame, fortune, and challenging combat drew him to Pandora.  
 

 

  
 

The skill tree:

  
     
  All this abilities can be unlocked starting to tier 1, each 5 points the succesive tier will be unlocked and the skills of that tier will be purchaseable. Each time you level-up in the game you'll earn 1 point to spend in the tree you prefer. The fist point will be given at LV.5 and not at LV.1 (you can upgrade each tree by a little, not just focusing on one)  
     
 

Guerrilla

This trees focuses on close-range combat and improves the turret's duration and damage capabilities.
Essentially it allows a player to drop the turret to let it support him while he closes distance to engage enemies.

Tier Skill LV Effects
1 Sentry 5 +1 shot per burst and +2s duration for sabre turret per level
1 Ready 5 +8% reload speed per level
2 Laser sight 5 +10% turret accuracy per level
2 Willing 5 +15% shield recharge rate and -12% shield recharge delay per level
3 Onlslaught 5 Kill Skill. killing an enemy gives you +6% gun damage and +12% movement speed per level for a short time
3 Able 5 Damaging an enemy regenerates 0.4% of your Maximum Health per second per level for 3 seconds
3 Scorched earth 1 Adds multi-rocket pods to your sabre turret. 22 rockets per volley
4 Grenadier 5 +1 grenade capacity per level
5 Crisis management 5 +7% gun damage and +6% melee damage per level when shields are depleted
6 Double up 1 Adds a second gun to the sabre turret and both guns fire slag bullets
 
     
 

Gunpowder

This tree focuses on medium range combat, providing many boosts to weapon damage of all kinds and general combat skills. Also allows for tactical deployment of the turret as "another soldier on the field" via the Longbow skill.

Tier Skill LV Effects
1 Impact 5 +4% gun damage and +3% melee damage per level
1 Expertise 5 +14% weapon swap and aim speed per level
2 Overload 5 +10% magazine size for assault rifles
2 Metal storm 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy gives +12% fire rate and +15% recoil reduction per level for a short time
3 Steady 5 +8% recoil reduction, +5% grenade damage, and +4% rocket launcher damage per level
3 Battlefront 5 +6% gun, melee, and grenade damage per level while turret is deployed
3 Longbow turret 1 +10000% Turret deploy range, +110% turret health
4 Duty calls 5 +5% gun damage and +3% fire rate with non-elemental guns
4 Do or die 1 Allows you to throw grenades while in fight for your life. +10% grenade and rocket launcher damage
5 Ranger 5 +1% gun damage, accuracy, critical hit damage, fire rate, magazine size, reload speed, and maximum health per level
6 Nuke 1 Deploying your sabre turret sets off a small nuclear blast
 
 

 

  
 

Survival

This tree is defense oriented, providing general defensive stat boosts as well as abilities that can pull the player back from the brink of death. It also improves the turret's deployment versatility.

Tier Skill LV Effects
1 Healthy 5 +6% Maximum Health per level
1 Preparation 5 +3% shield capacity per level; regenerate 0.4% health per second per level when shields are full
2 Last ditch effort 5 +8% Gun Damage and +14% Movement Speed per level during Fight For Your Life
2 Pressure 5 Up to +14% Reload Speed and -12% Shield Recharge Delay per level depending on how low your health is
3 Forbearance 5 -8% Status Effect duration on you and +1% Maximum Health per level
3 Quick charge 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy regenerates 1% of your shield per second per level for a short time
3 Phanlax shield 1 Your Sabre Turret projects a shield that attempts to block enemy ranged fire but lets friendly ranged attacks pass through
4 Resourceful 5 +5% Action Skill Cooldown Rate per level
4 Mag-lock 1 Your Sabre Turret can be deployed on walls and ceilings
5 Grit 5 You have a 4% chance per level to ignore damage that would otherwise kill you. In addition to not taking damage from the attack, you will also regain half your Maximum health
6 Gemini 1 Allows you to deploy two Sabre Turrets
