Whatever Krieg went through to make him the blood-drinking, meat-loving killer that he is, it is noted he wasn't always like this. Krieg's psyche has a remnant of who he used to be--a literal inner voice which forces Krieg to only kill the deserving, and help others. The voice often remarks that he remembers helping people, getting paid with loot, and other Vault-hunter-esque pursuits. It's also informed Krieg that should Krieg ever kill an innocent person, the inner voice will take over and commit suicide to kill them both as punishment.  
 

 

  
 

The skill tree:

  
     
  All this abilities can be unlocked starting to tier 1, each 5 points the succesive tier will be unlocked and the skills of that tier will be purchaseable. Each time you level-up in the game you'll earn 1 point to spend in the tree you prefer. The fist point will be given at LV.5 and not at LV.1 (you can upgrade each tree by a little, not just focusing on one)  
     
 

Bloodlust

Focuses on ranged combat using a Bloodlust stack mechanic. Dealing damage to an enemy adds a stack of Bloodlust to a maximum of 100. Stacks decay after 3 seconds if you haven’t dealt damage at a rate of 1 stack per second

Tier Skill LV Effects
1 Blood twitch 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy gives +0.3% Weapon Swap speed per Bloodlust stack per level. Dealing damage to an enemy adds a stack of Bloodlust. Stacks decay after a few seconds if you haven't dealt damage
1 Blood-filled guns 5 +0.5% Magazine Size per Bloodlust stack per level. Dealing damage to an enemy adds a stack of Bloodlust. Stacks decay after a few seconds if you haven't dealt damage
2 Blood overdrive 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy with a bullet gives +0.5% Melee Damage and -0.005s Grenade Fuse Time per Bloodlust stack per level for a short time
2 Bloody revival 5 +0.3% Assault Rifle damage during Fight For Your Life per Bloodlust stack per level
2 Taste of blood 5 +0.1% damage reduction during Buzz Axe Rampage per Bloodlust stack per level. +10 Bloodlust stacks per kill (+5 per level) during Buzz Axe Rampage
3 Blood bath 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy with a grenade or explosion gives +0.5% weapon damage per Bloodlust stack per level for a short time. Enemies killed this way have a 15% chance per level of dropping Grenade ammunition
3 Buzz axe bombardier 1 When thrown, your buzz axe now has Dynamite attached to it which explodes on impact
3 Fuel the blood 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy with a melee attack gives +0.2% Grenade Damage per Bloodlust stack per level for a short time and adds +2 Bloodlust stacks per level
4 Boiling blood 5 Increases the time before Bloodlust stacks start to decay by 0.5 seconds per level
4 Blood trance 5 Increases the duration of Buzz Axe Rampage by 0.05 seconds per Bloodlust stack (at time of activation) per level
5 Nervous blood 5 Kill Skill. Killing an enemy gives +0.3% Reload Speed per Bloodlust stack per level for a short time.
6 Bloodsplosion 1 Killing an enemy causes them to explode with an Elemental Nova matching the element of the damage that killed it (Non-elemental damage will cause an explosive nova). Overkill damage is added to this damage. Each Bloodlust stacks increases this damage by 5%

Getting a revive with the bundle explosion gives you bonus Movement Speed for the remainder of the time that was left in Light the Fuse.

Throw Dynamite sticks with Fire. Detonate bundle by holding the reload button

2 Pain is power 5 +5%, +10% Weapon and Melee Damage per level (except for Sniper Rifles). -5% Critical Hit Damage (-5% per level)
2 Numbed nerves 5 +10% damage reduction per level when on fire
